History Of Skateboarding

In the period of 1940s till 1960s, the first skateboards started with wooden boxes, or boards, with roller skate wheels attached to the bottom. It was an idea by Californian surfers in which they felt like doing something as similar as surfing when the waves were flat

It was called "SIDEWALK SURFING"

After a decade of the introduction of the skateboard, a lot had happened especially with the idea of using quality materials in order to make the skateboard. With different companies that had been established that had dedicated to making skateboard parts

Skateparks were built with ramps, halfpipes and many more for the skaters to play and perform tricks
